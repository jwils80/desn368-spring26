Lucille Ball

1911-1989

"I'm not funny. What I am is brave."

  • 1940 Lucy meets Desi Arnaz, and elopes after a year.
  • 1950 Lucy and Desi established their own studio and production company, "Desilu Productions".
  • 1951 "I Love Lucy" premiered. It was filmed with three cameras, on high quality film. Lucy and Desi helped fund this extra expense, and they retained ownership of the show, and recordings. This is considered one of the smartest business decisions in the Entertainment Industry.
  • "I Love Lucy" ran through 1957. Because of their show, they became TV's first millionaires, and then multimillionaires shortly after.
  • December 8, 1952, Lucy announced her pregnancy on the show. She was pregnant with her second child, Desi Arnaz Jr., and had them write the pregnancy into the show. At the time, this was unheard of, and Lucy became the first pregnant character in TV history.
  • 1953 One of the most viewed TV broadcasts in history was "Lucy goes to the hospital". This episode coincided with the actual birth of Lucy and Desi's son. 15 million more people watched this episode than watched President Eisenhower's inauguration the very next day.
  • 1956 "I Love Lucy" made a Christmas special, one of the first in history. It didn't air again for decades.
  • 2013 CBS airs Lucy's Christmas special, in a colorized format. 8.7 million people watched, and it has become a cult classic.

Lucille Ball was iconic. She didn't become a start until she was in her 40s, and she never gave up, even when people told her she wasn't good enough. She kept reaching for the stars, and shattered the glass ceiling, paving the way for the women that came after her.
