Intro

Chaos Momma is a maker. She works in fiber arts, paints, digital, sketch, and just about anything else she can get her hands on. Shes sees things differently than others. Where others see order and grids, she sees chaos and freestyle. Her work is vastly different from the traditional norm, but somehow, it works.

Work

Chaos Momma thrives on the different. Lives for the unusual. And relishes in the crazy. Her designs and creations show small windows into her chaotic brain.

Contact

Chaos Momma can be found on social media, with examples of her fiber arts, and she is working on other sites to showcase the rest of her chaos.
