Opening Bakery

  1. turn on all the things
  2. check for orders
  3. pull bread rack out of walk-on
  4. figure out what was pulled, decide what you're making
    • rolls and regular croissants go straight in proofer
    • stretch out french bread and score it, 4 on a tray, put it in proofer
    • make hoagies: stretch out french bread, and cut in half twice, first half the french bread, then cut the halves down the center. line the uncut end together, 12 on a tray
    • make any specialty bread. stretch french bread, spray with water, lightly sprinkle with seasoning. put in proofer, or set aside to be proofed after first round goes in oven. 4 on tray.
    • make rings. Cut french bread in half, down the middle, stretch and form a ring, spray with water, sprinkle with seasoning. put in proofer or set aside to proof after first round. 2 to tray
    • make twist. cut french bread in half down middle, then split the half almost to top. twist halves together, spray with water, and season. 4 to a tray.
    • make foccacia. split french bread almost in half, down the middle. spread out from middle to make it flatter and wider than french bread. spray with water, sprinkle seasoning. put in proofer or set aside to proof after first round. 2 on a tray.
  5. get first round of donuts in oven, bake for 8 minutes
    • long johns
    • rings
    • bavarian
    • persing (cinnamon rolls)
  6. glaze next couple rounds
    • old fashioned
    • rings
    • twists
    • raspberry filled
    • apple fritters
  7. get chocolate icing heated, ice long johns, rings, bavarian
  8. get maple heated, ice long johns, drizzle some chocolate long johns, ice persings
  9. get buttercream icing, ice long johns, pershings
  10. drizzle whats needed
  11. heat white icing, ice and dip rings in cereal. heat tie dye icing, ice rings.
  12. load donuts in donut case
  13. put in flavored croissants after bacon, if needed. steam setting, 15 minutes. glaze strawberry straight out of oven.
  14. put in first round of bread, rolls, french bread
  15. tell the other bakery person, if scheduled, what the plan for bread is. take a break (stagger with deli opener)
