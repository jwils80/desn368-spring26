Chaotic Mom Advise

Did your mom ever pass along advice to you? Did it ever make you wonder where the hell she learned the advice? Well...I'll fill you in on a little secret...The day your mom became a mom, her mind started to slowly go crazy. As she learned to parent, she learned little tidbits to pass on to other moms.

Here's some of the things I never thought I would say, and some advice I learned while raising my boys.

  • Whenever you change a diaper, lay the baby sideways to you...like a capital T...or you may wear what should go in the diaper.
  • Bathrooms are gross. especially with boys. And no, it probably won't change with age.
  • Farting and poop jokes will always be funny to boys.
  • "Don't do stupid shit" is something I say at least once a day.
  • Usually followed by..."If you do, don't get caught". Also followed with please don't make me have an er trip today.
  • "Did you die?" (yes stolen from someone funnier and smarter than me, but it tracks)
  • "Are you bleeding?"
  • "Wait, what? How? When...Nevermind, I don't want to know"
  • No you cannot clothes off, I know you're hot, but we are in a store, and not everyone wants to see you birthday suit.
  • "underwear, then pants."
  • People are thinking about you way less than you think. Not because you’re unimportant—because everyone else is busy overthinking their own lives. It’s a very inefficient system
  • Clean as you go. Future You does not deserve to walk into a disaster you created like a little chaos gremlin
  • You don’t have to have it all figured out. Honestly, most people are just confidently guessing. Some just have better outfits while doing it

Being a mom is fun, scary, tiring, draining, hard, and the best roll I've ever taken on. It's not for the weak, especially cleaning up after....anything. But, I love all the parts, even the ones that question my sanity, and leave me utterly speechless.
