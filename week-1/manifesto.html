Jwils Coding Manifesto

Coding is a new concept to me. Sort of. I took web development as my "intro to coding" when I should have taken web design. Oh well, live and learn.

Live and Learn

I've never stopped learning, and I don't plan to stop living anytime soon either. I'm determined to figure this out, even if it is just to use the tools at my disposal more efficiently.

Having Kids that are Smarter than Me

My children are great, and they are smart kids. It feels weird asking them for help with my schoolwork, but here we are in this digital freakshow.

We Were All Coders

Do you remember MySpace? I do. We were all coders then. We would reshape our top 8, or take them out altogether. We would add fun graphics, and music. We didn't know we were coding though. Again, live and learn.

Here We Are

We are now in the age of the in-between. I've been in the in-between my whole life, so it just makes sense to learn coding in between the "do it all yourself" stage and "AI is doing it all for us" stage.

Never Give Up

My new motto is "Never Give Up." Well, that and the snarky comments that constantly come flying out my mouth. And coffee. And chaos. I want to make all of those things work together. Here's to hoping!
