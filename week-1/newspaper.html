He is the Corporate Silence: Dread Him Not!

September 10, 1980
Jen Wilson

Residents and fans in a New York neighborhood are piping mad! They were all excited to rename the streets after the poet that had lived in a nearby farmhouse. Unfortunately important details were missed. The blame can be passed to any and all above.
What's the mistake you ask? Well, the poet's name is none other than Edgar Allan Poe. The street name however is Edgar Allen Poe. Residents are apalled, and upset, and demand rectificatition. The city has offered to glue 'a's' over the 'e's', But most residents say that that isn't good enough.

No word yet on the outcome.
