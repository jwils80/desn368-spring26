Big Mac Pasta Salad

This recipe is a twist on the classic Big Mac. Kids love it, and so do adults. Also yummy for leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 12 oz rotini pasta
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded iceburg lettuce (added before serving)
  • 1 cup french fried onions (added before serving)

For Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 2 tbsp dill pickle juice
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 cup chopped dill pickle

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef until browned. Drain and set aside to cool.
  2. Cook pasta according to box instructions (al dente).
  3. To make sauce: Whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, pickle juice. Add spices once combined. Stir in pickles, set aside.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine pasta, beef, and cheese. Mix until well combined.
  5. Add sauce to pasta mixture, stir until well combined. Cover and chill for 1-2 hours.
  6. Before serving, add lettuce and fried onions. Combine well, serve and enjoy.

Notes

  • Cheese doesn't have to be cheddar, it tastes good with colby jack, will probable taste yummy with whatever your favorite cheese is.
  • The onions are not mandatory, but add a nice crunch.
